Find a Movie!

This pages is used to help you find a movie from your favourite streaming service based off of the top movies on IMDB.

Pick a Platform:

To start find the streaming platform that appeals to you more using the graph picture below. For your convenience you can filter platforms using the "Platform Selection" drop down menu.




Pick a Movie

Now you can filter out the steaming service that you picked in the last step using the "Platform Selection" drop down menu under the scatter plots. Then hover over the point on the plot to pick the movie that appeals to you.

If your feeling adventurous randomly pick a point to find a movie to watch.
